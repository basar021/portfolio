Process

Why Hire Me?

1. Initial Meeting

At first I discuss with the client what kind of website he wants and I want some of his competitors website as a sample.

2. Planning

Then I try to understand his requirements deeply and make a plan how to make his project perfectly and uniquely.

3. Design & Development

Then I take the necessary content to him for creating the website and I design and develope the website with the best concentration.

4. Quality Testing

Then I test the function of the website such as the menus, buttons, form, links, compatibility, security is ok.

5. Submit for Review

Then I ask to the client to check the website and if any change or revision is required I do these happily.

6. Delivery

Finally I send the website with source code. And I make sure to give full support to the clients after the sell service.
